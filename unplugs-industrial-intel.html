üìã ANSI Standards

  • S3.19-1974: Still required for EPA labeling; generates NRR on packages
  • S12.6-2016: Modern standard with Method A (trained) and Method B (inexperienced)
  • Key Insight: Real-world protection is 25-60% of labeled NRR

  • 3M PELTOR is legacy infrastructure ‚Äî embedded in enterprise procurement, radio integration expertise, but slower innovation cycle.
  • "Aware" / situational awareness is the differentiator ‚Äî products with ambient passthrough command premium pricing and solve the #1 complaint: isolation from coworkers and alarms.
  • Worker acceptance is the real bottleneck ‚Äî comfort, fit, and communication ability matter more than NRR in actual adoption. The best protection unused is no protection.
